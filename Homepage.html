What we do

Our time capsule helps you to store a memory or something special that you want to be hidden away in our three proposed destinations for the future. User has to choose locations that are offered and with this there are various price tag associated to them as some are most scolder and has to pay via bitcoin transaction using QR code system.

How to use it

You have the choice between three of our proposed destinations for your capsule ,Underground of Earth , Orbiting the moon or even making it travel accross the universe.You can input any information of your choice that you want to be preserve for the future, for example your favorite photo , a video of an event you like or even your DNA.

How it works

After selection your desired capsule destination , you will be redirected to the package selection page where you have a wide variety of packages for the types of data you want to preserve. The capsule time can be set to return back according to your prefered choice.

Underground Earth

Your capsule will be hidden underground at your prefered location on earth.Our packages depends on the location (underwater , reserve place , etc..) and the time-stamp duration.

Around The Moon

Your capsule will be sent to orbit around the moon with your given data.

Across The Universe

Set your capsule on an adventure across the universe where the posibility of reaching another life form.
